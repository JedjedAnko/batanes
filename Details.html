Siargao Island's Most Beautiful Spots

  • Cloud Nine

    One of the best-known surfing spots in Siargao is Cloud 9, to which the town owes its surfing legacy. Cloud 9 is world-famous for its thick, hollow tubes and fast-barreling right-hand waves that break over a reef.

  • Sohoton Cove

    With crystal clear waters, a jellyfish sanctuary, gorgeous caves, and immense beauty, Sohoton Cove is a great place to spend a day.

  • Magpupungko Tidal Pools

    The Magpupungko rock pools are natural tidal pools found in the town of Pilar, on the east coast of Siargao.

  • Club Tara Resort

    Nestled in the tranquil island of Bucas Grande, Club Tara Island Resort boasts a private beach area, a restaurant and an outdoor swimming pool. Free Wi-Fi access is available in the public areas, while activities such as snorkelling and canoeing can be enjoyed on site.

  • Sugba Lagoon

    Sugba Lagoon is one of Siargao’s top rated destinations. Almost completely enclosed in a small island off of Siargao, the water in the lagoon is this outrageous blue-green. From the air, Sugba Lagoon resembles a strange maze of vibrant water separated by small islands and domed mountains.

  • Coconut Trees View

    One of the island’s most amazing tourist spots is an epic viewpoint looking over hundreds or thousands (probably way more) of coconut trees that go on right up into the mountains.
